How it works

Weekly meetings:

Each week you will meet with two other founders on zoom call.

You will tell them how you are getting traffic and leads.

They will tell you how they are getting traffic and leads too.

You will help each other to improve your lead generation process.

You will help each other to write better ads and how to improve your current ads.

You will tell them what are your current problems and they will advise you on how to solve them.
